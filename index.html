Our story

Coffee is a craft,
not a commodity.

We opened Ember in 2019 with one idea: that a great cup of coffee is worth slowing down for. Every bean we use is sourced directly from small farms, roasted in small batches, and brewed with care.

Whether you're here for your morning ritual or a long afternoon, we want every visit to feel like the best part of your day.
