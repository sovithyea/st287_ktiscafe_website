Our story

Coffee is a craft,
not a commodity.

We opened st287 with one idea: that a great cup of coffee is worth slowing down for. Every bean we use is sourced directly from small farms, roasted in small batches, and brewed with care.

Whether you're here for your morning ritual or a long afternoon under the mango tree, we want every visit to feel like the best part of your day.
